About this Qualification

The Level 3 Learning in the Outdoors Diploma is delivered by Agored Cymru. Learners apply knowledge and skills in complex outdoor activities, develop leadership, analyse risk independently, and take responsibility for their own and others' learning in the outdoors.

Mandatory Units

  • HB93CY003 — Risk Management when Learning in the Outdoors (3 credits)
  • HB93CY004 — Skills Development through Outdoor Learning (4 credits)
  • HB93CY002 — Sustainable Participation in Outdoor Activities (3 credits)

All three units must be completed and passed. They account for 10 of the 42 required credits.

This qualification supports all four purposes of learning — developing learners as:

  • Ambitious, capable learners
  • Enterprising, creative contributors
  • Ethical, informed citizens of Wales and the world
  • Healthy, confident individuals
A

Assessment

All units are internally assessed by your teacher (assessor) and internally verified by the centre. There are no exams. Evidence is gathered through written tasks, practical observation, portfolios, and leadership demonstrations.

Agored Cymru requires centres to complete external moderation to confirm standards.

Future Pathways & Career Opportunities

Level 3 opens direct routes into higher education, outdoor leadership careers, and professional adventure sector roles.

Outdoor Leadership
Mountain leader, kayak/canoe coach, climbing instructor, DofE expedition leader, forest school qualified practitioner
Adventure Sector
Activity centre manager, outdoor education coordinator, adventure tourism operator, survival/bushcraft instructor
Higher Education
Outdoor education degree, sport science BSc, environmental science, geography — Level 3 meets university entry requirements
Environment & Ecology
NRW ranger, conservation officer, National Park warden, habitat management, ecological survey work
Public Services
Mountain Rescue, RAF Mountain Rescue, coastguard, fire service swift water rescue, armed forces specialist roles
Education & Youth Work
Outdoor learning teacher, youth worker, community sport development, Scouts/Guides leadership, therapeutic outdoor practice
