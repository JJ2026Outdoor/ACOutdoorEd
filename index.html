About this Qualification

The Level 2 Learning in the Outdoors Extended Certificate is delivered by Agored Cymru and supports the Welsh Government's Curriculum for Wales. It develops learners holistically through experiential outdoor learning — building knowledge, skill, and confidence in real-world environments.

Mandatory Units

  • HB92CY002 — Personal Safety when Learning in the Outdoors (2 credits)
  • HB92CY003 — Sustainable Use of Natural Resources (1 credit)

Both units must be completed and passed to achieve any size of this qualification.

Links to Curriculum for Wales

This qualification supports all four purposes of learning — developing learners as:

  • Ambitious, capable learners
  • Enterprising, creative contributors
  • Ethical, informed citizens of Wales and the world
  • Healthy, confident individuals
✅

Assessment

All units are internally assessed by your teacher (assessor) and internally verified by the centre. There are no exams. Evidence is gathered through written tasks, practical observation, and portfolios of work.

Agored Cymru requires centres to complete external moderation to confirm standards.
