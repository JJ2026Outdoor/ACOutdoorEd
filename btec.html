About this Qualification

The Pearson BTEC Level 1 Introductory in Public Services is a practical, skills-focused qualification. It emphasises demonstrating what learners can do rather than theoretical knowledge, giving them the confidence and skills to progress to further study or training in the public services sector.

Assessment

All units are internally assessed by your teacher and internally verified by the centre. There are no exams. Each unit is graded at Pass, Merit or Distinction. Summative assessment takes place only after all teaching content has been covered.

Links to Public Services

This qualification supports learners exploring careers across the public services sector. Sector units cover fitness testing, navigation and adventurous activities — skills directly relevant to emergency services, armed forces, and outdoor public service roles.

Transferable Skills

  • Time management and organisation
  • Planning and research
  • Communication and teamwork
  • Problem solving in practical contexts
  • Self-review and reflection

Where Could This Lead?

Progression Routes

These qualifications prepare learners for further learning at a higher level in public services. Successful completion can lead to:

  • Pearson BTEC Level 2 in Protective Services or Security
  • Level 2 qualifications in Sport or other sectors
  • Apprenticeships and vocational training programmes
  • Other Level 2 study programmes

Public Services Careers

This qualification provides a foundation for learners considering careers across a wide range of public services, including:

  • Police, fire and ambulance services
  • Armed forces and military roles
  • Border force and customs
  • Youth work and community services
  • Outdoor education and expedition leadership

Progression Pathways

Further Education
Progress to BTEC Level 2 in Public Services, Protective Services, or Sport at college or sixth form.
Apprenticeships
Move into a sector apprenticeship in emergency services, the armed forces, or community roles.
Outdoor Education
Continue into outdoor education, expedition leadership, or Duke of Edinburgh programmes and qualifications.
Other Study Programmes
Transferable skills gained open doors to Level 2 programmes across a range of vocational sectors.
